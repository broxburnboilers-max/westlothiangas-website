2. Full Cover Plan — What Is Included

The Customer agrees to take out the Company's top-tier “Full Cover” plan at a fixed rate of £29.99 per month, which includes:

  • Everything in the Service & Breakdown plan (full annual boiler service, unlimited breakdown call-outs, parts & labour up to £500/repair, no excess fee, 12-month workmanship guarantee)
  • Full central heating system covered, including radiators, valves and the pump
  • Landlord Gas Safety Certificate (CP12) included, provided the required annual visit takes place and access is granted
  • Priority, top-of-queue response ahead of standard plan customers
  • 24/7 WhatsApp response for support and emergencies (07961 768920)
  • Ideal for landlords & rental properties, as well as homeowners

3. Minimum Term — 12-Month Lock-In

The Full Cover plan under this Agreement begins on the date of your first successful payment following signature (“the Commencement Date”) and continues for a fixed minimum term of 12 months (“the Minimum Term”).

By signing this Agreement, the Customer specifically agrees to be bound by this fixed 12-month term.

After the Minimum Term ends, the plan will automatically continue on a rolling monthly basis at the then-current published rate, unless the Customer gives notice to cancel or the parties agree a new fixed-term arrangement.

4. Fees and Payment

  • Monthly plan fee: £29.99, fixed for the entire 12-month Minimum Term and will not increase during that period
  • Payment method: recurring card payment collected securely via Stripe
  • Payment date: collected monthly from the date of your first successful payment
  • Late or failed payments may result in cover being suspended until the account is brought up to date, without affecting the Customer's ongoing liability for the remainder of the Minimum Term

5. Early Termination

If the Customer cancels the plan, stops payment, or otherwise ends this Agreement before the end of the Minimum Term for any reason other than those set out in Clause 6, the Customer agrees to pay the Company an early termination charge equal to the outstanding monthly fees (£29.99 × remaining months) for the balance of the Minimum Term.

This sum is due within 14 days of termination and may, at the Company's discretion, be reduced or waived in exceptional circumstances.

6. Exceptions — No Penalty Situations

The Customer may end this Agreement without an early termination charge if:

  • the property is sold and evidence of the sale/change of ownership is provided in writing; or
  • the gas supply to the property is permanently disconnected; or
  • the Company fails to carry out a material obligation under this Agreement and does not remedy this within 14 days of written notice from the Customer.

7. Gas Safety Certificate

Provided the annual service visit takes place and the Company is given reasonable access to the property, the Company will issue a Landlord Gas Safety Certificate (CP12) for the boiler as part of each annual service during the Minimum Term, at no extra charge.

8. Access and Customer Obligations

  • Provide, or arrange for the tenant to provide, reasonable access for the annual service and any breakdown call-outs
  • Keep the boiler and surrounding area accessible and free from obstruction
  • Keep contact details up to date and inform the Company promptly of any change of tenant or change of address
  • Notify the Company promptly of any suspected fault or gas safety concern

9. Exclusions and Waiting Period

  • A 14-day settling-in period applies from the Commencement Date before breakdown claims can be made. Annual services can be booked immediately
  • Faults identified during an initial assessment, if any, are excluded from this plan; faults that develop after the Commencement Date are covered in full under the plan terms
  • Damage caused by misuse, third-party interference, or failure to allow reasonable access is excluded
