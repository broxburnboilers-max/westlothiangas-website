Gas safety

Always use a Gas Safe registered engineer

Gas Safe Register is the official list of engineers legally allowed to work on gas appliances in the UK. Always ask to see the Gas Safe ID card and check the engineer or business on the Register before any gas work is carried out.

Check the ID card

Every Gas Safe registered engineer carries an ID card. Check the photo, expiry date and the categories of gas work they are qualified to do.

Check the Register

You can verify an engineer or business online using Gas Safe Register before work starts.

Landlord legal duties

Landlords must arrange an annual gas safety check for gas appliances and flues provided for tenants.

Know the warning signs

Yellow flames, staining around appliances, unusual smells or symptoms like headaches and dizziness can indicate a dangerous appliance.
