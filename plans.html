Common questions

Is there a waiting period before I can make a claim?

There is a 14-day settling-in period from sign-up before breakdown claims can be made. Annual services can be booked immediately.

Are pre-existing faults covered?

We carry out an initial assessment on sign-up. Faults identified at that point are not covered, but any new faults that develop after joining are fully covered.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. Simply message us on WhatsApp to cancel.

Do you offer a fixed-term plan for landlords?

Yes. As a landlord, you can choose to lock in our Full Cover plan at a fixed £29.99/month for a 24-month term on a simple signed agreement, with your rate guaranteed for the full term. This is a separate signed agreement that sits alongside — not instead of — our standard cancel-anytime plans above. Message us on WhatsApp to find out more.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all of West Lothian and Edinburgh, including Livingston, Broxburn, Bathgate, Linlithgow, Bo'ness and surrounding areas.

How do I contact you in an emergency?

Message us on WhatsApp at any time on 07961 768920. Emergencies are always our top priority.
